### Problem
Period facts are frozen into display strings:
- `PropertyAssessmentMap.jsx`: `<h1>` "…assessment, 2026", `<p>` "…over 365,406 properties", and the `PHASE1_STATUS §5` colour-scale numbers in the sidebar `<p>`.
- `choroplethStyle.js`: `STOPS` colour-scale values hardcoded (with a "DO NOT recompute per refresh" note).
- `ReportCard.jsx`: "Edmonton — 2026." headline.

After a refresh these read stale even when the data is current.

### Fix
Pull year, `n_properties`, and the colour-scale block from the manifest entry for the
active `(city, year)`. Decide explicitly whether `STOPS` stays locked across years or is
recomputed (the manifest carries the scale either way — this just resolves the "DO NOT
recompute" comment into a real, recorded decision).

### Acceptance
- [ ] Headline year + property count render from the manifest, not literals.
- [ ] Sidebar colour-scale figures render from the manifest's `colour_scale` block.
- [ ] A decision is recorded (CLAUDE.md or PHASE1_STATUS): colour scale locked vs per-year.
- [ ] 2026 renders identically to today after the change.
